## 🚀 Featured Work

### [Wind Farm Layout Optimization](https://github.com/pdwytr/Optimizing-Windmill-layout-Competition-by-Shell-Ltd)
**Shell.ai Hackathon · International Competition**

> Optimizing turbine placement to maximize energy yield while minimizing wake loss — a constrained spatial optimization problem at scale.

- Built custom optimization algorithms over geospatial terrain data
- Achieved **top ranking** among international teams
- Stack: `Python` · `Optimization Algorithms` · `Geospatial Analysis`

---

### [Walmart Sales Forecasting](https://github.com/pdwytr/Forecasting-sales-of-Walmart-retail-goods)
**M5 Forecasting Challenge (Kaggle)**

> Point forecasting across 42,840 hierarchical time series — 3,049 products × 10 stores × 3 states, 5 years of daily data.

- Ensemble of time-series models with cross-validation on multi-level hierarchies
- Designed to handle intermittent demand and holiday effects
- Stack: `R` · `LightGBM` · `Time Series Analysis` · `Ensemble Learning`

---

### [Human Activity Recognition from Video](https://github.com/pdwytr/Recognising-the-specific-activities-from-video)
**Computer Vision Research**

> Real-time classification of human activities from surveillance video — distinguishing sports, routine, and anomalous behavior.

- LSTM-based temporal model over CNN-extracted frame features
- Handles variable-length sequences and noisy surveillance input
- Stack: `Python` · `TensorFlow` · `OpenCV` · `LSTM`
